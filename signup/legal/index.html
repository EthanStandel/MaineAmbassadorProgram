Things to Understand
    Before Signing Up

    For now, all a user is required to understand, is that your data will be open to the public once you sign up for these services. It will be handed over to either an ambassador user (if you're a guest) or a guest user (if you're an ambassador) without any further requirements for agreement. This design was chosen to protect the purity of the Maine Ambassador Program as a friendly networking tool, and not as a "lead system."

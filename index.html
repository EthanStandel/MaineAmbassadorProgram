    If you are on vacation in Maine, you’re one step closer to living the way life should be. If you’ve already moved here, fantastic! We’re excited to already call you a Mainer. In either scenario, we’d love to connect you with an Ambassador and demonstrate what Maine is all about.

    Request your meet up with an Ambassador!

    The Maine Ambassador Program is simple. It is an application that connects two people; one interested in learning more about Maine and one interested in sharing information about Maine. Maine Ambassadors are simply Mainers who have signed up to welcome people to our State and guests are individuals who have decided they’d like to meet a local.

    The program was built by the young people of PROPEL. Led by Chris Lee and Eric Collins on the business side and Ethan Standel and Sean Campbell as the software engineers, they turned a vision of statewide ambassador program benefiting all Maine communities into a reality. Today the Maine Ambassador Program is a fully automated web application that connects people in Maine’s predominate tourist and population centers. The program acts as the boots on the ground for Maine’s efforts to help people make living and working in Maine a reality.

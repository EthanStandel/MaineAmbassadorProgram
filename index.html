The Maine Ambassador Program

    Ethan Standel & Sean CampbellDecember 2016

    The goal of the Maine Ambassador Program is to help connect visitors to Maine with a real vision of life in Vacationland.

    This is an application supported by local non-profit, PROPEL Portland. The application has been supported and built by local developers for the benefit of all of Maine & it's visitors.
